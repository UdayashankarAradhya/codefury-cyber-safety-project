Real-Life Scam Stories

These stories are based on real events reported in India. Learning from others' experiences can help you stay safe.

The Fake KYC Update Scam

A 59-year-old woman in Delhi received a WhatsApp message from a scammer pretending to be a bank official, claiming her KYC details were outdated. Panicked, she followed their instructions and downloaded a malicious app, losing ₹47 lakh.

The Online Investment Scam

A man in Bengaluru was introduced to a "guaranteed high-return" Bitcoin investment platform by someone he met online. After seeing initial fake profits, he invested more and more, eventually losing ₹44 lakh.

The "Easy Money" Job Scam

A woman in Bantwal received a Telegram link promising high daily earnings for simple tasks. After paying "task fees" that kept increasing, she lost over ₹1.7 lakh without ever being able to withdraw her supposed earnings.
