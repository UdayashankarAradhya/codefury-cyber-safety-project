Understanding Online Scams

Knowledge is the best defense. Choose a profile to see targeted tips and examples.

Common Scams Everyone Should Know

Phishing

Fraudulent messages impersonating trusted services to steal credentials.

How to avoid: verify sender, avoid clicking unknown links.
UPI / Payment Scams

Fake requests, QR codes, or reversed payment traps.

Tip: never enter your PIN to receive money.

Scams Targeting Students

Fake Part-time Jobs

Requests for payment to get a job, or using your account for transfers (money-mule).

Avoid: never pay to get hired. Ask for employer verifiable details.

Scams Targeting Teachers

Fake Government Grants

Unsolicited links asking for application fees; check official .gov.in sources.

Scams Targeting Seniors

Emergency / Relative Scams

Scammers create urgency claiming a friend/relative needs money.

Always call the relative on a known number to confirm.

Scams Targeting Professionals

Investment & Trading Scams

Guaranteed returns or fake platforms showing fabricated profits.

Only use verified brokers and do independent checks.
