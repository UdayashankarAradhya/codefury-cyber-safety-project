🚨 Common Scams Everyone Should Know

💳 UPI/Digital Payment Scams

Scammers trick you into sending money by pretending to be someone trustworthy or creating fake emergencies.

📱 Fake OTP/SMS Scams

Scammers send fake messages claiming to be from your bank, asking you to share OTPs or click malicious links.

🎰 Fake Lottery & Prize Scams

You receive messages saying you've won a huge lottery or prize from companies you never entered.
